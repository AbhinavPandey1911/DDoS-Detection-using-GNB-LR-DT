A Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack is a malicious attempt to disrupt normal traffic of a targeted server, service, or network by overwhelming it with a flood of internet traffic. DDoS attacks can vary in scale and sophistication, ranging from simple volumetric attacks to more complex application-layer attacks.

Machine learning techniques can play a crucial role in mitigating the impact of DDoS attacks. By analyzing network traffic in real-time and leveraging historical data, machine learning algorithms can identify abnormal traffic patterns associated with DDoS attacks and trigger automated response mechanisms to mitigate the attack.
